❓ Aksar Puche Jane Wale Sawal

Indian passport ke liye photo ka size kitna hona chahiye? ▼
Indian passport ke liye photo 35mm × 45mm (physical print) ya 200×230 pixels minimum (online upload) honi chahiye. Online upload ke liye file size 10 KB se 1 MB ke beech honi chahiye.
Kya ghar par passport photo banai ja sakti hai? ▼
Haan! White background ke saamne seedha khade hokar achhi light mein photo khicho. ToolNest se resize karo — bilkul studio jaisi photo banega! Online application ke liye ghar ki photo perfectly chalti hai.
Passport photo mein glasses allowed hain? ▼
Nahi! 2018 se India mein passport photo mein glasses allowed nahi hain — chahe prescription glasses hon ya sunglasses. Glasses utarke photo leni hogi.
Passport Seva portal par photo upload nahi ho rahi? ▼
2 common reasons: (1) File size 1 MB se zyada hai — ToolNest se compress karo. (2) Format JPEG nahi hai — PNG ya WEBP ko JPEG mein convert karo. ToolNest dono kaam karta hai!
Passport photo background kaunsa color hona chahiye? ▼
Sirf plain white background accepted hai Indian passport ke liye. Off-white bhi acceptable hai lekin pure white best hai. Colored ya patterned background se application reject ho sakti hai.
