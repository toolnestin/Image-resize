📌 50KB Kyun Zaroori Hai?

Government websites aur recruitment portals par photo upload ki limit hoti hai kyunki:

  • Unke servers par storage limit hoti hai
  • Slow internet users ke liye loading fast hoti hai
  • Database mein efficiently store hoti hai
  • Official processing mein asani hoti hai
